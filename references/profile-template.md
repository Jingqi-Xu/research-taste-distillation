---

## Usage Notes

- Every field must be supported by paper evidence; no unsourced judgments allowed
- When information is insufficient, leave blank and annotate rather than fabricate
- Core philosophy patterns must pass triple verification (see extraction-framework.md)
- At least 3 anti-patterns, at least 1 pair of internal tensions
- At least 3 specific honest boundary items
